About Me

I'm an IT student currently diving deep into the world of Data Analysis. My foundation is in Database Management. I actually enjoy the "logical puzzle" of cleaning up messy data and building optimized SQL schemas. For me, a solid database isn't just about storage; it’s the essential starting point for finding the stories hidden in the numbers.

While I spend a lot of time with ERDs and database administration, my real goal is to bridge the gap between technical structures and actual insights that help people make better decisions. I’m always looking to sharpen my skills and learn new ways to turn complex data into something meaningful.
