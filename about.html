About Me

As an IT student specializing in Database Management, I focus on the structural integrity and efficiency of data systems. I enjoy the challenge of taking unorganized information and transforming it into optimized relational schemas. My technical toolkit is centered around SQL, where I practice writing complex queries, stored procedures, and triggers to ensure data consistency and high performance.

Beyond just writing code, I am passionate about database administration and the logical puzzle of ERD (Entity-Relationship Diagram) design. I believe that a strong database is the foundation of any successful software, and I am committed to mastering the art of managing complex data environments.
